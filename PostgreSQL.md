## Name Origin
The name is a mathematical pun.

> PostgreSQL was originally called "*Postgres*", and was based on another database, "*Ingres*".
>
> In mathematics, there is:
> - "**Pre**fix notation"
> - "**In**fix notation", and...
> - "**Post**fix notation".
